    HRMS Solutions

    HRMS (Human Resource Management System) software is a comprehensive web based tool designed to streamline and automate various HR processes within an all scales-type organization.HRMS software serves as a centralized database for storing employee information, including personal details, contact information, job history, performance reviews, and salary details. HRMS software aims to enhance HR processes, improve employee engagement, and optimize workforce management for organizations of all sizes.

    Key Features:-

    • Recruitment & applicant tracking
    • Onboarding
    • Time & attendance tracking
    • Payroll Management
    • Performance Management
    • Training & development
    • Admin Benefits
    • Employee self service
    • Analytics and reporting
    • Compliance management
    • Integration capabilities and many more custom development
